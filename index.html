Computer Science and Engineering Department:

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering is one of the newest departments of the Faculty of Electronic Engineering, but it bears great responsibilities in graduating qualified engineers in the field of computers, theoretically and practically. The department includes a distinguished group of faculty members who hold a doctorate from various European and American scientific schools in various fields of computer science. The faculty members in the department are responsible for everything related to the field of computers and their applications within the college and university through the scientific and administrative supervision of the courses. Who teaches at the Information Technology Club, the Scientific Computation Center, and the mechanization of business, the administrative work of the college, and he also takes the responsibility of supervising the university's network of connectivity and information. As well as supervising the establishment and implementation of the electronic portal project for the university and all colleges and institutes of the university.

Computer Science and Engineering Program:

The Computer Science and Engineering program is one of the undergraduate programs at the Faculty of Electronic Engineering - Menoufia University, where graduates of the program are awarded a bachelor's degree

Graduates of this program occupy sensitive jobs in various sectors of society, such as

  • establishing and operating wired and wireless networks, whether private or in the government sector.
  • Designing websites
  • Creating databases for hospitals, pharmacies, banks and schools
  • Designing software for multimedia transmission
  • Encrypting wired and wireless networks
  • Applications of microcontrollers in various factories
  • Implementation and operation of software for major companies such as Microsoft, Oracle and Cisco.
